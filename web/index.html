Boids Flocking Simulation

An interactive flocking simulation built with Macroquad (WASM).

A desktop version is available here.

Controls

  • Left Click — Spawn a predator at cursor position
  • Space — Spawn 50 new boids
  • R — Reset and clear all entities
  • E — Toggle mouse chase mode (boids flee from cursor)

How It Works

  • Each boid (white triangle) follows three simple rules:
    • Separation — Avoid crowding nearby boids
    • Alignment — Match the velocity of nearby boids
    • Cohesion — Move toward the center of nearby boids
  • Predators (red triangles) hunt and chase boids within their detection range
